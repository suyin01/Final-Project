Energise with Power. Detox with Hot. Stretch with Basics. Relax with Yin.

Power

A dynamic flowing class in a balmy 28 degrees. A faster placed class to tone and sculpt your body while synchronising movement with breath to leave you energised and rejuvenated.

Hot

A detoxifying and revitalising class. The heat is turned up to 35 degrees for a slow flow. Hot allows your body to stabilise, find balance and release while promoting the elimination of toxins.

Basics

A class to strengthen and stretch in a regular temperature room. Basics focuses on correct alignment to build a strong foundation. Perfect for those new to yoga or wanting a slower paced class without the heat.

Yin

Yin yoga poses are long, deep holds to provide enhanced mobility and relaxation. A blissul class to breathe and unwind in a welcoming, warm room.
